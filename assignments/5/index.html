Pick a Day:
The above map shows in what areas the most robberies occur in San Fransisco. Selecting the various days will show the reported locations where robberies occurred and the areas will be recoloured depending on the amount of robberies that occurred in them. If you quickly look through the days you will notice that the areas mostly keep their crime ranking. The park area has very few robberies and the central area has many. However you can see how the northern harbor area experience more robberies during Fridays and Saturdays. Remember that the datasets spans 12 years. There are not 600 robberies every Friday in central San Fransisco - rather there is an average of about one a day. Judging from the "crime" dots, most robberies occur along the main streets like mission street and market street.
