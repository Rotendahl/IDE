The population in a country is rarely steady. As opportunities arise in the cities and the countryside offers fewer jobs it is common for people to move towards the cities. In Denmark anno 1801 almost 80 % of the population lived in the countryside but in 2004 more than 85 % were found in cities. This is a massive shit over 200 years. What we want to show is that the population have not stabilized. People are still moving and small towns are suffering from business closing and jobs moving away. In recent years we have seen attempts at revitalizing "udkantsdanmark" (outer Denmark) by moving government jobs away from Copenhagen
